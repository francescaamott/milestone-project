After years running a small business and being a corporate retail manager, I am at last turning my love of travel into a career. Currently taking a professional development certification course in sustainable travel destination management through George Washington University, as well as a web design & development course, my goal is to help small businesses become successful participants in the sustainable travel movement.

My academic background comprises a bachelor's degree in international studies & economics and a master's degree in international communication, all from American University. I've spent the past ten years in the wellness & beauty industry, and completed a course in holistic nutrition. An avid traveler, I am a member of the Travel Massive networking community and write my own travel blog.
